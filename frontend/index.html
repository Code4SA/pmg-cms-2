Overview of Fourth Parliament

2014 marks the twentieth year of democracy, and the end of the Fourth Parliament in South Africa. This overview highlights aspects of the work done by Parliament between 2009 and 2014. This is not intended to be a comprehensive analysis, but a broad sweep over some of the matters that occupied Parliament, consideration of whether it achieved selected objectives, and will offer some suggestions on the likely strategy of the incoming Fifth Parliament after the elections.
